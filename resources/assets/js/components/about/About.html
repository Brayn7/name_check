Purpose:

NameSpy Provides a quick way for non-profit organizations to check and store a list of individuals or organizations that receive international monetary transactions.

How:

We provide a place to store recipient names in your user profile. Once the recipient is in the database we keep an eye on the SDN (Specially Designated Nationals) list and send you an email if there is someone you should be aware of. Signup here.
